Movie Recommendations

My Top 3 movies

1.Inception

This masterpiece directed by Christopher Nolan features Leo Di caprio and is going to leave you questioning your reality.

2.Five Feet Apart

A modern day rom com featuring Cole Sprouse who suffers from a lung disorder. Keep your tissues handy.

3.Interstellar

One of the highest rated science fiction films for a reason. You will be sitting at the edge of your seat throughout this.
